Introduction to Jacob Cannamela

I grew up in the small town of Waxhaw, North Carolina, just outside the bustling city of Charlotte. Although I loved the quiet charm of my hometown, I quickly grew to dislike the chaos and heavy traffic of the city whenever I visited. Those experiences made me appreciate the peaceful life in Waxhaw even more.

At the age of 15, I got my first job at Dominos Pizza, which taught me the value of hard work and responsibility. Unfortunately, during this time, I broke my ankle skateboarding, which sidelined me from delivering pizzas. A regular customer, who I often gave discounts to, took pity on my situation and suggested I try IT for his company in the city. That opportunity changed everything for me.

In 2014, I did a one-day trial run at the customers company and was hired as a part-time intern the very next day. That role sparked my career in IT and opened doors I never imagined. Fast forward to today, and I now work in cybersecurity as a Threat Detection Engineer, with a decade of experience in the IT industry. Its been an incredible journey of growth and learning.

Outside of work, my hobbies revolve around spending quality time with my family and exploring the latest advancements in technology. Both give me a sense of purpose and excitement as I continue to pursue new challenges and opportunities.
